How does the solar panel work in cloudy climates? ▼
The solar panels work in diffuse/cloudy light as well, though at reduced efficiency. The cameras also have a built-in rechargeable battery (approximately 6,200mAh) that provides backup power. In regions with consistently low sunlight (Pacific Northwest, etc.), you may need to recharge the battery every few weeks via USB-C — far less frequently than a camera without solar. In sunny climates, the panels maintain full charge indefinitely.
How difficult is the setup? Do I need an electrician? ▼
No electrician needed. The cameras are fully wireless and battery/solar powered — no wiring at all. Most users complete the full 4-camera setup in under 30 minutes. The aosuBase hub connects to your router via ethernet for reliable connectivity.
