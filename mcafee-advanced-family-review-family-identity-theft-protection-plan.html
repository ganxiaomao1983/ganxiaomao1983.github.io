What it is

What is McAfee+ Advanced Family?

McAfee+ Advanced Family is an all-in-one security suite that combines antivirus, a secure VPN, identity monitoring, identity theft insurance, and scam protection into a single, family-focused plan. Instead of buying separate products for security, privacy, and identity, a household manages everything under one subscription.

At a high level, the plan groups its features into a few modules: device security (antivirus across your computers and phones), online privacy (VPN and privacy tools), and identity protection (monitoring, alerts, identity theft insurance, and restoration support). The sections below walk through each one using McAfee's published descriptions and independent reviews.

What's included

What does the McAfee family plan include?

The McAfee+ Advanced Family plan bundles five main areas of protection — device security, online privacy, identity monitoring, identity theft insurance, and family member coverage — so the whole household is covered from one place. Here's what each part means in practice.

Device security

Keeps the family's devices protected day to day. McAfee+ Advanced Family includes cross-platform antivirus that helps protect Windows, macOS, Android, iOS, and ChromeOS devices in real time.

Privacy & VPN

Helps each person browse more privately on home and public networks. Every family member gets their own secure VPN and privacy tools to help keep their browsing and personal data safer online.

Identity monitoring

Watches for signs that a family member's information is exposed. McAfee+ monitors sensitive details like Social Security numbers, contact info, and financial accounts for signs of identity theft, using dark web scans, credit monitoring, and account alerts.

Helps with the cost of recovering if identity theft happens. Family member coverage

Built so each person has their own protected profile. McAfee+ Advanced Family is typically designed to protect up to two adults and four children under 18, giving each member their own login and tailored identity monitoring experience.

These reflect what many reviewers and some customers report — not universal facts.

Pros reviewers highlight
