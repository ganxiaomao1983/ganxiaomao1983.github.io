Our Verdict: Is McAfee+™ Premium Individual Worth It in 2026?

In 2026, the digital threat landscape has evolved beyond simple virus definitions. Identity theft and sophisticated SMS phishing are among the most common threats consumers face. McAfee+™ Premium Individual is positioned as a broad all-in-one bundle, combining device protection, a VPN, identity monitoring, and privacy tools under a single subscription — aimed at users who prefer one provider over several separate services.

The real-world tradeoff is performance and notification volume in exchange for breadth of coverage. Independent testing shows a lengthy initial full scan and a VPN that adds noticeable latency, and user feedback echoes frequent alerts. None of that is disqualifying, but it does mean McAfee+ suits people who want a full-featured, always-visible suite more than people who want something they'll forget is running.

The identity monitoring and recovery support are the strongest parts of the package: dark web scanning, breach alerts, up to $2 million in identity theft coverage, and access to licensed recovery experts who help you through the reporting and correction process. Coverage per McAfee's official plan page. Subject to terms and conditions. Verify at mcafee.com.

On price: the introductory terms (official 1-year or the featured 2-year deal) are close on a per-year basis, so the decision mostly comes down to whether you want to commit for two years upfront and whether you're comfortable managing the eventual renewal at $149.99/year.
